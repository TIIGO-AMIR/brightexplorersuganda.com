Booking Terms & Conditions

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Payment

A 30% deposit is required within 7 days of booking confirmation. The remaining balance must be paid 45 days before departure. For bookings made within 45 days, full payment is due immediately. We accept Mobile Money, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfers. All prices are in US Dollars unless otherwise stated.

2. Cancellation & Refunds

  • Cancellation more than 60 days before departure: full refund minus $100 admin fee.
  • 59-30 days: 50% refund of the total package cost.
  • 29-15 days: 25% refund.
  • 14 days or less: no refund.
  • Gorilla permits and flight tickets are non-refundable once purchased.

3. Changes by the Client

Any amendment to a confirmed booking is subject to an amendment fee of $30 per change, plus any additional costs from suppliers. Major changes (e.g., changing dates) are treated as a cancellation and rebooking.

4. Insurance

Travel insurance covering medical evacuation, trip cancellation, and loss of personal property is mandatory for all participants. We require proof of insurance upon booking.

5. Liability

Bright Explorers Uganda acts as an agent for hotels, airlines, and other suppliers. We are not liable for any injury, loss, or damage caused by third parties, force majeure, or government travel advisories.

6. Passports & Visas

Clients are responsible for ensuring they have valid passports and the necessary visas for Uganda. We can provide invitation letters for visa support on request.

7. Complaints

Any complaints must be reported immediately to our office or guide so we can resolve them promptly. Written complaints should be submitted within 14 days of trip completion.
