Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you make an inquiry or booking. This includes your name, email address, phone number, passport details (for bookings), and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and confirm your bookings.
  • To communicate with you about your trip and send pre‑departure information.
  • To send promotional emails about new safaris or offers, if you have opted in.
  • To improve our website and customer service.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share necessary information with trusted third‑party suppliers (hotels, lodges, gorilla permit offices) solely to fulfil your booking. All third parties are required to keep your data confidential.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience (e.g., remembering your preferences). You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly.

5. Data Security

We implement industry‑standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure servers, and restricted access to personal data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Under Uganda’s Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@brightexplorers.co.ug.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
